Things To Do

Take a ride on the Rail Runner

Built in 2006, the Rail Runner commuter train originally went from Belen to Bernalillo, just north of Albuquerque, with stops in Los Lunas, Isleta and Albuquerque. Later, it was extended all the way to Santa Fe. It soon became popular with college students and those who worked in Albuquerque. Schools would use it for field trips, and families would use it for trips to the Albuquerque Bioparks, Old Town and museums, as the train station is within walking distance.

Through the Flower Art Gallery

Did you know that Judy Chicago moved to Belen a few years ago? She opened up a gallery just west of the Harvey House Museum in the historical district between First and Second Streets. Judy Chicago's art is adult themed and tends to be a litte racy, so you might want to visit first before taking children into the gallery to make sure the content is appropriate for your children. The historical district of the city has lots of places to visit and things to see.

Through The Flower art Gallery focuses on art specifically pertaining to women.

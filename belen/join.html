Join the Chamber!


There are many benefits to joining the Chamber of Commerce. It is a built-in support system for small businesses.

1. Publicity Boost

We will help give your business more exposure online. Every time the Chamber mentions your business, you may get yourself a free link on other websites, which will give you a higher rank on Google, making your business more likely to show up on web searches.

2. Networking Opportunities

You will get to mingle with and get to know other business owners in your community. This will give you exposure and allow you business opportunities you wouldn't otherwise get. It also gives you advanced notice of expos and community events.

3. Access to Mailing Lists

Every member of the chamber gets a quarterly mailing list that includes everyone else who is a member of the chamber. This gives you opportunities to collaborate with other businesses in the community, or get in touch with other businesses who might be able to use your services.

4. Freebies and Discounts

Chamber of Commerce events often include mugs, sun caps, pens and other gifts that are given away as gifts or prizes. Often, businesses will raffle off or give away in a free drawing one of their services or products as a way to gain exposure. You may be lucky enough to win something.

5. Access to Funding

The chamber works directly with governmental and educational organizations. This gives you access to information you may need, such as business loans or legislation that affects you.


Membership Levels and Perks


Non-profit--$100 annually.

Visibility

Great for solo entrepreneurs. 1 representative is registered as a Chamber member.

We will announce your business on the welcome page of our e-newsletter and Facebook page.
Your business name, contact information and social media links posted on our website.
You can post your events on the Chamber's Calendar of Events.
You will get a physical sticker to place on the window of your business, as well as access to Chamber Artwork.
You will receive an invitation to the annual business expo.
You will recieve half-price booth rates at town events.

Connections

Quarterly multi-chamber events where you can network with members from other regional chambers.

Community Resources

Monthly newsletter highlighting all community and chamber events.
Discounts on all Chamber-sponsored community events.
Certificate of Origin for international trade.

Bronze--$200 annually.

Great for small to medium sized businesses.Up to 3 representatives per business. Includes all nonprofit benefits in addition to the following:
Ribbon cutting and grand opening promotion and assistance.
Free inclusion of your marketing materials in new resident welcome bags.
Allowed to hold special after-hours events.
Mailing list for marketing purposes.

Silver--$400 annually

Best for medium to large companies. Up to 5 representatives per business. Includes all nonprofit and bronze benefits in addition to the following:
Highlighted once annually on the Chamber facebook page.
Free standard booth at community events.
Free mailing labels for your marketing purposes.
Four free tickets to the annual awards event.
Silver Investor plaque for displaying in your business.

Gold--$1000

Best for large companies.Up to 10 representatives per business. Includes all nonprofit, bronze and silver benefits in addition to the following:
First opportunity to sponsor chamber events.
Free premium booth at all community events.
Your logo will be placed on chamber website pages, event banners and event t-shirts.
Six free tickets to the annual awards event.
Opportunity to present one award at the annual awards event.

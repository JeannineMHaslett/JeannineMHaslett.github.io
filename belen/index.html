Upcoming Events

Click section headings for a link with more info.

20th Annual Matanza

For 20 years, Belen has boasted the world's largest Matanza. It will be held at Eagle Park on January 23, 2021 from 7:00am to 3:00pm. Entry fee is $10 per person. Register to cook or sponsor the event.

35th Annual St. Patrick's Day Balloon Rallye

The 35th annual St. Patrick's Day Balloon Rallye will be held at Eagle Park on Saturday, March 13, 2021 from 7:00am until 12:00pm. Admission is free. There will be food trucks available for your convenience.

Belen Farmer's Market opening in June

The Belen Farmer's market will be opening up again beginning June 18, 2021 and will be held every Friday at Anna Becker Park from 4:00 to 7:00pm through October 29, 2021. Visitors may purchase locally grown eggs, honey and produce.

Rio Abajo Becker Street Arts Festival

Saturday, September 25, 2021 from 10:00am to 10:00pm. Enjoy music by local musicians, art by local artists, performances by schools, dance studios and Karate dojos, and lots of food.

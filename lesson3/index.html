Site Plan

Description

The purpose of this site section is to explain how the Weather to Weather website will look. It Includes the name and purpose of the site, color and typography choices and user personas to show what type of person whould be interested in visiting the site.

Site Name

The name of this site is Whether to Weather. The site lets users see the current weather, upcoming events and what the weather will be like that day. It will let them know whether or not the weather will be good on the day they plan their outing--whether they should take a sweater or coat, an umbrella or a hat and sunscreen.

Purpose Statement

There are many things that this site does. It allows users to check the weather forecast for the day and also the week. There is a community page where people can see what activities are going on in their area. Being able to check the weather forecast for that day helps people plan ahead. There is also a photo gallery where users can see photos of places around their area and see the activities of former community events.

Molly


Stay at home mom

Occupation:Molly is a 29-year-old stay at home mom with three active boys. She is always looking for activities that they can do during the week.



Education:She has an Associate's Degree in Early Childhood Multicultrual Education, and is currently attending college online for a Bachelor's Degree in Kinesiology.



Motivation for using the site: When her husband is homoe on weekends, the family likes to do things outdoors and they are always looking for community events to attend; whether it is a rodeo, First-Responder meet-and-greet, a fishing derby, or anything else that will get the children outdoors. Having a site where she can look for activities as well as check the weather for that day will help her to know whether she needs to take jackets or hats and sunscreen.



Social:Molly is a very social person and loves to be involved in the community, and have her children involved in community events, as well. She is always looking for fun activities as well as service projects that all of the members of her family can do together.



Technology:Molly has a Samsung Galaxy S10 smartphone with an unlimited data plan. She does most of her internet searches and social media activities using her phone during the day or when she is out of the house. She also has a laptop that she uses for her online schooling. She uses it occasionally for internet searches and social media.



Quote:Being a mom is the most important job I can do at this time in mhy life. I want to teach my boys everything they need to know about family life and life outside of the home so that they can grow up to be strong, confident men who are good members of society.



Zack


Heavy equipment mechanic/welder



Occupation:Zack is a 33-year-old Heavy equipment mechanic and welder. He works for a company that services broken-down heavy equipment like tractors,cranes, bulldozers, etc. He works mostly all day out doors because when tractors breakdown it's usually out in rural areas.



Education:Zack has a Welding certificate from Central New Mexico University, and a CDL from the New Mexico Department of Transportation.



Motivation for using the site:Zack drives all over the state, so it may take 4 hours of driving to get to one site. He often checks the weather forecast so that he knows whether to take a coat and gloves or not, and he likes to check announcements and traffic so that he doesn't end up getting caught in traffic jams for extended periods of time.



Social:He spends much of his time talking to customers and working with others. He has a few close friends that he and his wife like to spend time with on the weekends, and they like to go to activities like swap meets and take their kids to carnivals.



Technology:Zack uses an old iPhone 5 SE for all of his communications, internet searches and social media activity.



Quote:I like working outside. It beats staying inside and having to wear a mask. When I'm out there in the boonies all by myself, I can breathe the fresh air and think.

Scenario 1:

Q: What features are there in the weather center?


A: There is a storm center that displays weather alerts along with road closures


It has been snowing all day while Jimmy is at work. he has a 45 minute commute home, and there is a good amount of snow. He checksthe weather center to see where the snow iw the heaviest and if the roads are clear.



Scenario 2:

Q: How can I find out what's going on in the community?


A: Click on the community center and find a list of activities going on for the next few months in your area.


Alice loves to get out into the community and do things with her friends. She has some Paid Time Off so She checks the community section of the website to browse a list of activities coming up in the next three months. so she canmake an itinerary.

Q: I missed the fun run for the zoo. are ther any images or videos from the event?
